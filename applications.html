About Us

JengaITHub is an initiative that aims to provide space, skills and resources to make sure that every person interested in exploring the world of constantlly evolving technologies has the opportunity to do so therefore they can start developing the required skills and attitude early enough and build their careers.

Our Approach

Loss of talents in organisations and companies due to lack of career development has been a great challange. Our approach is to inspire and provide appropriate mentorship so that we can have individuals that are self-empowered and can navigate throughout their careers withought quiting or changing it. We also aim at empowering innovation because its the only way we can create revolutionists.

The Process

For a successful transition and realisation of talents, we considers carrying out the following proccess before fully accepting an individual into the programs;

  1. Register interest through application
  2. Attend a workshop - get information on the courses and assess your interests
  3. Join the program of choice if you are convinced its the appropriate one
  4. Graduate with the required skills and mindset
  5. Join the market and start solving real world problems
